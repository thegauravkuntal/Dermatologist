What is Botox®?

A purified protein that relaxes muscles to reduce wrinkles and prevent new lines.

Smooth. Prevent. Refresh.

Botox® ek purified protein hai jo targeted facial muscles ko relax karta hai. Isse na sirf purani jhurriyan (wrinkles) kam hoti hain, balki naye lines banne se bhi rukti hain. Ye treatment fast hai aur isme downtime bilkul nahi hai.

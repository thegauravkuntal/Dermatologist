Medical Precision

Lesion Removal

Safe and effective removal of benign skin lesions using advanced Hyfrecation.

What is Hyfrecation?

Hyfrecation ek minor medical procedure hai jo low-powered electrical current ka use karta hai benign (non-cancerous) skin lesions ko safely hatane ke liye.

Ye unwanted tissue ko precision ke saath gently cauterise aur destroy karke kaam karta hai.

What can be treated?

  • ✔ Skin tags
  • ✔ Seborrhoeic keratoses
  • ✔ Cherry angiomas
  • ✔ Dermatosis papulosa nigra
  • ✔ Warts (selected cases)
  • ✔ Small benign moles

Procedure Overview

• Local anaesthetic may be used depending on lesion

• A fine probe delivers controlled electrical energy

• Lesion is cauterised and removed

⏱ Time: 10–30 minutes

What to expect After

  • Small scab forms and falls off within 7–14 days.

  • Mild redness or pigmentation change may occur temporarily.

  • Minimal downtime; normal activities resumed same day.

Why choose this?

✨ Quick, precise, and effective

✨ Minimal bleeding due to cauterisation

✨ Low risk of infection

✨ Suitable for multiple lesions in one session

Meet

Dr. Sumaira Muneer

With over 15 years of extensive clinical experience, Dr Sumaira Muneer offers a refined, globally informed approach to dermatology and aesthetic medicine. Her career spans multiple international healthcare systems, providing advanced expertise in diagnosing and managing a wide spectrum of skin conditions across all skin types and ethnicities.

She specialises in seamlessly integrating medical dermatology with cutting-edge aesthetic treatments, delivering outcomes that are both scientifically robust and aesthetically sophisticated. Her practice is grounded in precision, safety, and a deep understanding of facial harmony.

Her areas of expertise include advanced injectables, skin rejuvenation, anti-ageing strategies, and medical-grade skin therapies, all performed using evidence-based protocols and the latest innovations in the field.

Her aim is to redefine personalised skin care by combining clinical excellence with aesthetic artistry—enhancing natural beauty, restoring skin health, and delivering subtle, elegant, and enduring results that inspire confidence.
